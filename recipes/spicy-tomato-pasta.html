Description

Sauce is made by frying onions in a pan till golden.

Then adding tomato paste and chilli paste to fry for another 5 mins.

  • 100 gm pasta
  • tomato paste
  • onion
  • chilli flake
  • salt
  1. Boil pasta till al dente, save a cup of pasta water
  2. Add oil to a saucepan and fry onions under low heat until golden
  3. Then add tomato paste and chilli flakes to saucepan
  4. Add cream and stir till homogenus
  5. Add pasta water to thicken the sauce accordingly
  6. Salt to taste
  7. Add cooked pasta to sauce
  8. Mix and serve
