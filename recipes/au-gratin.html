au gratin recipe

Simmered in the creamy sauce, the potatoes soften to the point of nearly melting, creating a texture that crosses the layered goodness of scalloped potatoes with the best cheesy mashed potatoes you've ever had. And, at Easter, the richness pairs perfectly with the sweet glazed ham or bitter greens on your plate.

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Cheddar
  • Milk
  • Flour
  1. Shave potaoes in mandolin
  2. Make roux with butter, flour and milk. Then add cheese to make cheese sauce of desirable consistency
  3. Thinly slice onions
  4. Butter a casserole and lay shaved potaotes on the bottom
  5. Follow by laying onions and another layer of potatoes
  6. Next, Pour over cheese sauce. Repeat process until casserole is full
  7. Cover casserole with aluminium foil and bake for 1.5 hr at 400-degree F
